# Sticky Notes Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Sticky Notes ("the extension") does **not** collect any personal information.

## 1. Data storage

- Notes you write (text, position, size, color, visibility scope) and extension
  settings are stored only in Chrome's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- All data stays on your device, inside your browser profile.

## 2. Data transmission

- The extension never transmits any data to external servers.
- It makes no network requests and contains no analytics, advertising, or
  tracking components.

## 3. Access to page content

- The extension's content script runs on pages only to display your notes. It
  does not read, collect, or modify the content of the pages you visit.

## 4. Data deletion

- Each note can be deleted at any time with its delete button.
- Uninstalling the extension removes all stored data.
